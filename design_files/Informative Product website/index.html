Do More Own Less

Share Shed is a non-profit company which allows its members to borrow items from their library for $80 per year in form of memberships.

Join Us Now!

How it works...

Share Shed runs on an annual membership basis. You can join online for $80 a year. You can also purchase a gift membership for someone else! We are not-for-profit so all membership fees go towards running Share Shed, fixing items or acquiring new ones.

​

Members can borrow up to five items per week for a seven day period. If you need more than 5 items at a time (maybe for a backyard birthday party or a family camping trip) we allow additional items to be borrowed for $5 each.

We have hundreds of items in the catalogue, including kayaks, family tents, lawn mowers and more. Some larger items attract a small additional borrowing fee to cover maintenance costs.
